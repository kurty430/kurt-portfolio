About

I like taking a manual, paper-trail process and turning it into something that just works — quietly, in the background, without anyone having to think about it.

That's been the throughline of my work at The Orchard Golf and Country Club: replacing spreadsheets and walk-in requests with systems that track themselves. I handle system administration and technical support day to day, and build the tools in between — from a full helpdesk platform to Excel automation for the accounting team. Before that, I designed and built websites at a digital agency, so I bring both the engineering and the interface side to everything I build.
